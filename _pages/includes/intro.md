# 🧑 About Me

<div class='paper-box-text' style="font-size: larger;" markdown="1">

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student at [Shanghai Jiao Tong University](https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/), where I began my doctoral studies in September 2022 and expect to graduate in June 2027. Previously, I received my bachelor's degree from [Xidian University](https://www.xidian.edu.cn/) (Sep. 2018--Jun. 2022). During my undergraduate years, I received 10 national awards and more than 15 provincial- and ministerial-level awards. I also led two national innovation projects and obtained three software copyrights.

My research interests include multimodal large language models, parameter-efficient fine-tuning, Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) architectures, and medical imaging. If you are interested in **academic collaboration**, please feel free to contact me at jackchenruan@sjtu.edu.cn.

My long-term collaborators at Shanghai Jiao Tong University include [Suncheng Xiang](https://jeremyxsc.github.io/), [Mingye Xie](https://myronxie.github.io/), and [Jingsheng Gao](https://gaojingsheng.github.io/). Beyond these long-term collaborations, I have also built research and industry experience across several institutions. I was previously advised by [Yu Cheng](https://ych133.github.io/) and [Xiaoye Qu](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=rT3hqdcAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN&oi=ao) at Shanghai AI Lab. At the Shanghai Institute for Algorithmic Innovation, I worked under the guidance of [Zhiyu Li](https://openreview.net/profile?id=%7EZhiyu_li2). I also had the opportunity to work with [Ye Guo](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=19fqrVQAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN) at Xiaohongshu. During my time at ByteDance, I was fortunate to be mentored by [De Cai](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=zyVRiAcAAAAJ&hl=en), [Fubang Zhao](https://openreview.net/profile?id=~Fubang_Zhao3), and [Yangyang Kang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=zh-CN&user=EuJk27UAAAAJ&view_op=list_works&sortby=pubdate).

In my free time, I enjoy swimming, unarmed fitness, music and guitar.
